Our Favorite Sites:

    • Nikon

      Nikon is a manufacturer and retailer of cameras, camera lenses, binoculars, and other imaging products. We can't speak to their cameras, but we know that their binoculars are good!

      Nikonusa.com

      Audubon

      Audubon gives news about birds, and has lots of guides for birding, if you're new to all things birds!

      Audubon.org

      BirdCast

      BirdCast website gives bird migration forecasts, and live bird migration maps, so you know where to find them.

      BirdCast.info

      UtahBirders

      UtahBirders is a website that tries to be an all-in-one website for birding in Utah. It's not quite there, since it doesn't have online guides, but it does do most everything else!

      UtahBirders.com

      eBird

      eBird is a log of bird sightings from the Cornell Lab of Ornithology. If you click on the name of a bird, you get information about it, such as how endangered it is. On the birds page, you can also access a gallery for that specific bird

      eBird.org
